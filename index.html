About

I am a PhD candidate in statistics, studying on the EPSRC StatML CDT at Imperial College London and the University of Oxford. I was previously at the University of Cambridge, where I studied Mathematics, with a focus on theoretical physics, particularly fluid dynamics. I am currently working under the supervision of Dr Edward Cohen, Dr Francesco Sanna Passino, and Dr James Martin. My primary area of research is into network point processes, though I also have experience with spatial point processes. Recently, I have worked on developing online methodology for detection changepoints within the latent structure of network point processes, utilising a Bayesian framework.
